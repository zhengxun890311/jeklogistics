  • Live Entry

  • Ensure your shipments move swiftly with our live entry customs clearance services. Live entry filing expedites the clearance process by submitting entry documentation as soon as your cargo arrives at U.S. borders. Our experienced team works in real-time to manage required filings, coordinate with U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP), and address any potential issues immediately. Trust us to provide seamless, fast-tracked customs solutions that minimize delays and keep your supply chain moving efficiently.

  • ATA Carnet

  • Simplify your international trade process with our expert carnet customs clearance services. A carnet, also known as a “merchandise passport,” allows for the temporary importation of goods without paying duties or taxes in participating countries. Our team specializes in managing carnet documentation, ensuring compliance with international regulations, and facilitating smooth border crossings. Trust us to handle the complexities, so you can focus on expanding your global reach with confidence and ease.
