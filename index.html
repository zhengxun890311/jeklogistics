  • Ensure your shipments move swiftly with our live entry customs clearance services.   • Simplify your international trade process with our expert carnet customs clearance services. A carnet, also known as a “merchandise passport,” allows for the temporary importation of goods without paying duties or taxes in participating countries. Our team specializes in managing carnet documentation, ensuring compliance with international regulations, and facilitating smooth border crossings. Trust us to handle the complexities, so you can focus on expanding your global reach with confidence and ease.

  • Simplify customs clearance with our expertise in T&E (Transportation and Exportation) and IT (Immediate Transportation) filings. What's the current MPF(Merchandise Processing Fee) rate ?

The MPF rate for formal entries is 0.3464% of the entered value of the merchandise, with a minimum and maximum fee ($29.66 minimum and $575 maximum, as of 2025)

What's the current HMF(Harbor Maintenance Fee) rate ?

The HMF rate for formal entries is 0.125% of the entered value of the merchandise is only for ocean shipment and there is with no minimum and maximum fee.
